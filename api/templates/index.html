Magic Music Mapper



Magic Music Mapper is an innovative web application that creates personalized Spotify playlists based on your emotions. Powered by Artificial Intelligence, our app discerns the intricate nuances of your emotions, translating them into a curated selection of tracks from Spotify's extensive library. Each playlist is a symphony of emotions, a musical portrayal designed to complement your current state of mind. It offers a unique way to discover new songs or revisit old favorites, all in tune with how you feel. With Magic Music Mapper, immerse yourself in a musical journey tailored to your emotional landscape, making every listening experience uniquely yours.
